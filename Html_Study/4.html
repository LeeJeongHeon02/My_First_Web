lotteria

Lotteria Co., Ltd. (株式会社ロッテリア, Kabushiki-gaisha Rotteria) is a Japanese company that operates a chain of fast food restaurants in East Asia, having opened its first restaurant in Tokyo in September 1972. Taking its name from its parent company, Lotte Corporation, it currently has franchises in Japan, South Korea, Indonesia, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, and Myanmar.[1] The origin of the name is a combination of corporate names Lotte and Cafeteria.
